Biosketch:

I have completed my bachelor's degree in Electronics Engineering from Mumbai University. I did my undergraduate thesis on 'Virtual Self-Driving Car' under Prof. Sejal Kadam. Thenceforth, I worked on many projects for autonomous driving using deep learning and computer vision frameworks.

My research interest primarily lies in autonomous vehicles, focused on perception tasks and vehicle motion control. Using open-source simulators such as CARLA & Udacity SDC to implement various computer vision techniques, also deploy & evaluate machine learning models and path planning algorithms for autonomous driving. I look forward to implement sim2real transfer of trained Reinforcement Leaning driving policies to bridge the gaps that exist between dynamics of a simulator and real-world environment.

I like to read and learn more on creative applications of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning and the wide set of possibilities it presents.
