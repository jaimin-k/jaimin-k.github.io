PedesTrain: Pose Detection & Awareness Prediction of Pedestrains

Using pose detection module of Mediapipe, detecting pose of pedestrains. Possible pedestrian actions[using mobile phones, phone call while walking, aware while using phone] are recorded as batches of video, frames recorded for different actions is used as training data. Using facial and pose landmarks from the trained model, estimating the level of awareness based on the actions of pedestrains as the approach near to vehicle with installed ADAS.

Number plate recognition using OpenCV
